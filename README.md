Xbox Game Pass Sales Analysis

This project consists of a detailed analysis of Xbox Game Pass subscription sales, focusing on identifying revenue patterns, plan preferences, and the impact of additional services such as EA Play and the Minecraft Season Pass.

📌 Project Overview

The primary goal is to transform raw subscriber data into strategic business insights. Through data processing, we answer critical questions regarding total revenue, auto-renewal behavior, and the sales performance of bundled products.

📊 Data Description

The project utilizes a dataset structured into four main components:

    Bases (Subscribers Data): Contains detailed records for each subscriber, including:

        Subscriber ID: Unique identifier.

        Plan: Type of plan (Core, Standard, Ultimate).

        Subscription Type: Frequency (Monthly, Quarterly, Annual).

        Auto Renewal: Automatic renewal status.

        Add-ons: Indicators for EA Play and Minecraft Season Pass ownership.

        Total Value: Total transaction value, accounting for coupons and extras.

    Calculations (Business Intelligence): Consolidation of business metrics and formulas.

    Assets: Visual definitions, including logos and the official color palette (Xbox Green, Dark Grey, etc.).

    Dashboard: Visual structure for presenting sales KPIs.

🚀 Business Questions Answered

The project was built to address the following business inquiries:

    BQ1: What is the total revenue from annual plan sales?

    BQ2: How is the annual plan revenue split between subscriptions with and without auto-renewal?

    BQ3: What is the total sales volume generated specifically by EA Play subscriptions?

    BQ4: What is the total sales volume generated by the Minecraft Season Pass?


📈 Expected Results

The project demonstrates the revenue distribution across monthly, annual, and quarterly plans. Additionally, it highlights the significant revenue increment driven by the EA Play Pass and Minecraft Pass add-ons.
